About Us

Dolores O'leary is a one of a kind. She has sailed around the world, lived in 17 countries and now dedicates her life to creating the most unforgettable experiences money can buy. Whatever your need Dolores has the means. From flare throwers to world class dj's, master whiskey blenders to award winning chefs, nothing is too much to ask at this catering service. Capino provides the scene and Dolores calls on her expertise and vast knowledge in the catering and entertainment indsutry. Aboard Capino dreams come true and Delores is there to make sure that happens. Welcome aboard Capino and be prepared for the time of your life.

Packages

Champagne Testing

To not drink champagne is to forego life's joys. And what is better than to know more about that which provides so much joy. Whether interested in learning more about champagne or there to enjoy the festivities this is an experience to be savoured. Our selection of champagnes guided by our master sommelier.

Degustation Menu

Why eat one meal when you can eat many? And this is exactly the idea behind our degustation menu. We invite award winning chefs to prepare a menu consisting of a number of small dishes to delight the senses. We also pair the food with wine and champagne on request.

Whiskey Tasting

Our Master whiskey distiller is on hand to take you through some of the most exciting whiskies in the world. Here you learn how to distinguish the different characteristics and nuances of the whiskeys selected for the evening. Upon request we also do guest selected whiskeys.

DJ Dance Party

What better way to celebrate than to dance the night away with an award winning dj on your own boat. We will rig up the bar with exotic cocktails and flambouyant bartenders to keep you moving. Hors d'oeuvre are served throughout the night and of course we can cater to anything else your heart desires.
